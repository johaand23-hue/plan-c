Our Story

A table set
like a kitchen
at home.

Tucked below Brattle Street in the heart of Harvard Square, Alden & Harlow is a subterranean tavern built on the belief that great food doesn't announce itself. Chef Michael Scelfo cooks the way he feeds his family — with honest ingredients, deep technique, and no performance.

The room is dark. The wood is old. The drinks are serious. And everything on the plate is exactly what it needs to be — nothing more.

— Chef Michael Scelfo
